ETC

Electronic Toll Collection System

At present the collection of user fee for the use of the Expressway is done manually at the toll booth in cash. The payment of the user fee can now be done electronically using the new Electronic Toll Collection System called "ETC"

Electronic Toll Collection or "ETC" is the most cost effective and convinient way to pay for your user fee.
